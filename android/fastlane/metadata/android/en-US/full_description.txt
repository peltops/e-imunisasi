e-imunisasi is a mobile application designed for families and healthcare workers to simplify the immunization and vaccination process for infants and young children.

Features:
- Immunization schedules and history tracking
- Appointment booking with clinics
- Quick check-in to clinics
- Digital immunization record book
- Reminders for upcoming vaccinations

This app helps parents keep track of their children's immunization schedules, ensuring they receive all necessary vaccines at the appropriate times.
